Daily Greens

Daily Greens, an organic green juice, has grown from being in 400 stores to 1000 stores in the past year. Their rapid expansion has been met with multiple product line extensions, which has resulted in the need for packaging. Working closely with the Daily Greens team, I have created multiple packaging solutions that have helped secure regional distribution deals and elevate the brand in the national market. A few of these packaging designs are still in production and will be added to the website soon.

Print and Non-Traditional

Because of their rapid expansion, Daily Greens has asked me for a variety of non-traditional advertising, print advertising, infographics, and promotional materials. These materials are distributed nationally in retail outlets, festivals, promotional events, email blasts, and social media.
