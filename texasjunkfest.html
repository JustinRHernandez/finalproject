TEXAS JUNK FEST

Role: Art Director, Photographer, Production Designer

Print Advertising, Web Design, Art Direction, Outdoor Ambient, Social Media, Promotional Materials

Daily Greens, an organic green juice, has grown from being in 400 stores to 1000 stores in the past year. Their rapid expansion has been met with multiple product line extensions, which has resulted in the need for packaging and print advertising. Working closely with the Daily Greens team, I have created multiple packaging solutions that have helped secure regional distribution deals and elevate the brand in the national market. A few of these packaging designs are still in production and will be added to the website soon.
